A powerful, fully automated WordPress plugin designed to streamline content curation and creation. This plugin fetches the latest news from targeted websites via RSS feeds or HTML scraping, processes the raw content using OpenAI’s GPT models, and automatically publishes unique, SEO-friendly articles directly to your WordPress blog.

### Key Features & Capabilities
- **Multi-Source Fetching:** Supports scraping and fetching from multiple URLs simultaneously with a primary RSS fetch and a fallback to raw HTML scraping.
- **AI-Powered Rewriting:** Automatically rewrites post titles and body content to ensure 100% unique, plagiarism-free, and SEO-optimized articles using GPT-3.5/4.
- **Smart Summarization & Formatting:** Generates a concise 4-line summary and automatically structures content into a "Case Study" format for better readability.
- **Automated Media Handling:** Intelligently extracts featured images from source URLs and sideloads them directly into the WordPress media library.
- **Duplicate Prevention & Automation:** Built-in safeguards check source URLs to prevent publishing duplicate content. Fully configurable via WordPress Cron for hands-off, continuous background operation.
- **Category Auto-Detection & Logging:** Automatically categorizes fetched posts and maintains a comprehensive activity log for easy debugging and tracking.

### Impact / Outcome
- Drastically reduced content creation time from hours to zero.
- Enabled websites to scale their content output effortlessly while maintaining high SEO standards and unique readability.
- Provided a seamless, native WordPress admin interface, making AI integration accessible without needing complex external SaaS tools.
